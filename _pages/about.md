About Me
------
I am a MASc student in Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering at University of Waterloo. I am also a member of [Mechanical Systems Control Lab](https://uwaterloo.ca/waterloo-mechanical-systems-control-lab/), where I am advised by Professor [Soo Jeon](https://uwaterloo.ca/mechanical-mechatronics-engineering/profile/soojeon). Before this, I completed my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

My research interests lie on the intersection of robotic manipulators and autonomous vehicles, particularly in the areas of control, perception, and machine learning. Currently, I am working with SLAM to enhance perception and navigation in uncertain environments.
