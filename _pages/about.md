I am a senior majoring in Mechanical Engineering at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/). I am interested in applying control and optimization theories to robotic manipulators and autonomous vehicles. My research interests include control, perception, and machine learning. Through my research, I would like to enable manipulators to perform complex tasks and ensure the safe and efficient navigation of mobile robots in uncertain surroundings.

Currently, I am doing research on motion control of a dual-arm robot TIAGo++ under guidance of [Dr. David Navarro-Alarcon](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/me/people/academic-teaching-staff/david-navarro-alarcon-dr/) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Additionally, I am also conducting my Final Year Project on a delivery mobile vehicle with an active suspension system with [Dr. Kar Hang Chu](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/me/people/academic-teaching-staff/chu-kar-hang-henry-dr/) at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I had the opportunity to work on several projects relating to mechanicsm design, robot perception, and manipulation.
